This is Our Story

Easy discovery & booking platform for Unique stays & Exciting things to do across India at low prices with best service

From 2012 to 2017, Our leadership team worked with many corporates, colleges, schools and institutions to organise adventure group trips and in these 6 years we understood HOTELS are old and unconventional and people are looking for more experimental places to spend their vacations but hard to discover. After intensive ground work for more than 2 years, we discovered a lot of Camping Sites, Igloos, Homestays, Cottages exist at most uncrowded and virgin destinations , which can't be discovered if you have never been there. We thought to make these stays available by making it more organised keeping in mind amenities, safety and cleanliness. And Voila! Travel took Birth after nurturing it for more than 2 years.
